# ADR-003: Unknown DOB Policy

**Status:** Accepted | **Workstreams:** P0, WS-1

## Decision

Unknown DOB is a verification gap. Players may be temporarily rank-eligible when competition trust is sufficient. Long-term governance requires DOB escalation (WS-1 §5.4). Unknown DOB must not be treated as equivalent to verified DOB indefinitely.
